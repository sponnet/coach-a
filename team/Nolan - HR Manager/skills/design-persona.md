# Skill: Design a New Team Member Persona

## Trigger

Use this skill when you receive a Skills Profile from Pax and need to turn it into a complete AI team member.

## Steps

1. **Read the Skills Profile** — absorb all competencies, mental models, and recommended persona traits.
2. **Choose a name** — short (one word), easy to address, memorable, not already in the roster.
3. **Write the identity block:**
   - Name, Role title, Reports-to (always Larry unless specified otherwise)
4. **Write the persona paragraph** — who this person is, how they think, what drives them.
5. **Write communication style** — bullet list: tone, format preferences, any signature habits.
6. **List core capabilities** — what they can do, mapped directly from Pax's hard-skills list.
7. **List boundaries** — what is explicitly out of scope (keeps roles clean and non-overlapping).
8. **Draft sample output** — one short example of the kind of deliverable they produce.

## Quality checks

- Does the persona feel like a real specialist, not a generic assistant?
- Is the name unique in `team/agent-index.md`?
- Are capabilities scoped tightly enough that this hire doesn't overlap with existing team members?
- Is there at least one clear boundary ("does not do X")?

## Output

Populate the rich contract template at `team/<Name> - <Role>/AGENTS.md`.
