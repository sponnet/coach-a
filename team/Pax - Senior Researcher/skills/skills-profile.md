# Skill: Produce a Skills Profile

## Trigger

Use this skill when Larry needs a Skills Profile before Nolan can hire a new specialist.

## Steps

1. **Understand the domain** — what field or role is being researched? Get this from Larry's brief.
2. **Research the domain** — use WebSearch and WebFetch to find:
   - Job postings for senior practitioners in this role
   - Industry frameworks, certifications, or methodologies
   - Communities, blogs, or thought leaders in the space
3. **Structure the profile:**

### Domain Overview (2–3 sentences)
What is this field? What problem does it solve?

### Core Competencies (Hard Skills)
- Must-have technical skills (mark with ★ for top-priority)
- Tools and platforms commonly used
- Frameworks or methodologies

### Supporting Skills (Soft Skills)
- Communication, collaboration, and thinking styles that distinguish great from average

### Mental Models & Decision Frameworks
- How do experts in this field reason? What lenses do they apply?

### Practitioner Levels
- Junior vs. Mid vs. Senior — what separates them?

### Common Pitfalls
- What do average practitioners get wrong that experts avoid?

### Recommended AI Persona Traits
- Tone, communication style, working philosophy that would suit an AI in this role

## Output

Return the structured Skills Profile to Larry (or directly to Nolan if Larry routes you there). Keep it tight — no padding, no generic filler.
